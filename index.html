GETTING TO KNOW EACH OTHER

The goal of this exercise is to meet people you haven’t met yet, and get to know them a little better in 3 easy steps:

1. Find a partner and stand still

2. Ask you partner one of the two questions on your card, and answer one of the two questions on their card. Try to do this in less than one minute

3. Exchange your cards and find a new partner.

TOP TIPS

Be flexible: adapt your question if you notice it’s difficult to answer.

5. Click the card deck to stop your turn, and click again to start with the next player.

Pay attention to the time limit (60’’), so that you talk to as many people as possible.
